# TechNova Solutions Pvt. Ltd.

# Vendor Management Guidelines

**Policy Version:** 2.0
**Department:** Operations and Procurement
**Effective Date:** January 2026

---

# 1. Purpose

This policy defines standards for vendor onboarding, evaluation, monitoring, and management.

Objectives:

- Ensure vendor quality
- Reduce operational risk
- Improve compliance
- Optimize procurement processes

---

# 2. Scope

Applicable to:

- Suppliers
- Contractors
- Service providers
- Technology vendors
- Consultants

---

# 3. Vendor Lifecycle

```text
Vendor Identification
         ↓
Evaluation
         ↓
Approval
         ↓
Contract
         ↓
Performance Monitoring
         ↓
Renewal/Termination
```

---

# 4. Vendor Categories

Types of vendors:

- Technology vendors
- Hardware suppliers
- Software providers
- Cloud providers
- Service providers
- Consulting firms

---

# 5. Vendor Evaluation Criteria

Evaluation factors:

- Financial stability
- Technical capability
- Market reputation
- Security compliance
- Experience
- Cost effectiveness

---

# 6. Vendor Registration

Required documents:

- Company registration certificate
- PAN/GST certificate
- Bank details
- Tax documents
- Compliance certifications

---

# 7. Security Assessment

Technology vendors must provide:

- ISO certifications
- Security reports
- Compliance documents
- Data protection agreements

---

# 8. Service Level Agreements

Vendor contracts must include:

- Availability targets
- Performance metrics
- Response times
- Escalation procedures
- Penalties

---

# 9. Vendor Performance Review

Review frequency:

| Vendor Type | Review Frequency |
|-------------|-----------------|
| Critical | Quarterly |
| Standard | Half-yearly |
| Low Risk | Annually |

---

# 10. Risk Management

Vendor risks include:

- Financial risk
- Security risk
- Operational risk
- Compliance risk
- Legal risk

---

# 11. Vendor Termination

Termination reasons:

- Contract violation
- Poor performance
- Security breaches
- Fraud
- Business closure

---

# 12. Audit Requirements

The organization may conduct:

- Vendor audits
- Security audits
- Financial audits
- Compliance reviews

---
