# TechNova Solutions Pvt. Ltd.

# Leave and Attendance Policy

**Document Version:** 1.0
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026
**Department:** Human Resources
**Applicable To:** All Employees

---

# 1. Purpose

The purpose of this Leave and Attendance Policy is to establish a standardized framework for managing employee attendance, leave entitlements, leave approvals, and workplace attendance expectations at TechNova Solutions Pvt. Ltd. This policy aims to promote employee well-being while ensuring business continuity and operational efficiency.

---

# 2. Scope

This policy applies to:

* Full-time employees
* Probationary employees
* Contract employees
* Remote employees
* Hybrid employees

The policy does not apply to external consultants or temporary agency workers unless otherwise specified in their contracts.

---

The standard working schedule at TechNova Solutions is:

| Parameter            | Details            |
| -------------------- | ------------------ |
| Working Days         | Monday to Friday   |
| Office Hours         | 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM |
| Daily Working Hours  | 8 Hours            |
| Weekly Working Hours | 40 Hours           |

# 4. Attendance Requirements

Employees must:

* Report to work on time.
* Record attendance using the official attendance platform.
* Notify managers in case of delay or absence.
* Maintain professionalism and punctuality.
* Adhere to assigned work schedules.

Repeated absenteeism or late arrivals may result in disciplinary action.

---

# 5. Leave Categories

Employees are entitled to various types of leave as outlined below.

## 5.1 Annual Leave

Annual leave is intended to support employee work-life balance and personal well-being.

### Entitlement

| Employee Type         | Annual Leave    |
| --------------------- | --------------- |
| Full-time Employee    | 12 Days         |
| Probationary Employee | Pro-rated       |
| Contract Employee     | As per Contract |

### Rules

* Annual leave accrues monthly.
* Leave requests should be submitted at least two working days in advance.
* Maximum carry-forward is six days.
* Carried-forward leave expires on March 31 of the following year.

---

## 5.2 Sick Leave

Employees are entitled to sick leave when they are unable to work due to illness.

### Entitlement

* 10 sick leave days per calendar year.

### Conditions

* Sick leave cannot be encashed.
* Sick leave cannot be carried forward.
* Medical certificates are mandatory for absences exceeding three consecutive days.
* Managers may request medical documentation for extended absences.

---

## 5.3 Casual Leave

Casual leave is intended for unforeseen personal matters.

### Entitlement

* 5 casual leave days annually.

### Rules

* Casual leave cannot exceed three consecutive days.
* Unused casual leave expires at year-end.
* Casual leave cannot be converted into annual leave.

---

## 5.4 Maternity Leave

Female employees are entitled to maternity leave according to applicable labor regulations.

### Entitlement

* Up to 26 weeks of paid maternity leave.

### Eligibility

* Employees must have completed at least 80 days of employment prior to the expected delivery date.

### Benefits

* Full salary continuation.
* Medical insurance coverage.
* Job protection during approved leave.

---

## 5.5 Paternity Leave

Eligible employees may avail paternity leave following childbirth.

### Entitlement

* 10 working days of paid leave.

### Conditions

* Must be utilized within six months of childbirth.
* Supporting documentation may be requested.

---

## 5.6 Bereavement Leave

Employees may request bereavement leave in the event of the death of an immediate family member.

### Entitlement

* Up to 5 working days.

Immediate family includes:

* Spouse
* Parents
* Children
* Siblings
* Grandparents

---

## 5.7 Emergency Leave

Emergency leave may be granted for unforeseen circumstances.

Examples include:

* Family emergencies
* Medical emergencies
* Natural disasters
* Personal crises

Approval is subject to manager and HR review.

# 7. Leave Approval Process

Employees must follow the leave approval workflow:

### Step 1

Submit a leave request through the HR portal.

### Step 2

Immediate manager reviews the request.

### Step 3

HR verifies leave balance and eligibility.

### Step 4

Final approval notification is issued.

Emergency leave requests may be processed retrospectively.

---

# 8. Work From Home Attendance

Employees working remotely are required to:

* Log in by 9:00 AM.
* Maintain online availability during office hours.
* Attend scheduled meetings.
* Submit daily work updates.
* Record attendance using company systems.

Failure to comply may affect performance evaluations.

---

# 9. Late Arrival Policy

Employees arriving late are expected to inform their manager immediately.

### Penalties

| Occurrence          | Action          |
| ------------------- | --------------- |
| First 3 occurrences | Verbal warning  |
| 4-6 occurrences     | Written warning |
| More than 6         | HR review       |

Persistent lateness may impact performance ratings.

---

# 10. Unauthorized Absence

An unauthorized absence occurs when:

* An employee fails to report to work.
* No prior approval is obtained.
* No notification is provided.

### Consequences

* Salary deduction.
* Formal warning.
* Disciplinary proceedings.
* Potential termination for repeated violations.

---

# 11. Leave Encashment

Annual leave encashment is subject to company policy.

### Rules

* Maximum encashment: 5 days.
* Encashment occurs during final settlement.
* Casual and sick leave are not eligible for encashment.

---

# 12. Exceptions

The Human Resources department reserves the right to approve exceptions under special circumstances including:

* Medical emergencies
* Natural disasters
* Government restrictions
* Business continuity requirements

# 13. Frequently Asked Questions

### Q1. Can annual leave be carried forward?

Yes. Employees may carry forward up to six days.

### Q2. Can sick leave be encashed?

No. Sick leave cannot be encashed.

### Q3. Can I apply for leave after taking it?

Only in emergency situations with manager approval.

### Q4. What happens if my leave request is rejected?

You must report to work or submit an alternative request.

### Q5. Can I combine multiple leave types?

Yes, subject to manager and HR approval.
