# TechNova Solutions Pvt. Ltd.

# Meeting Guidelines and Communication Standards

**Policy Version:** 2.0
**Department:** Operations and Human Resources
**Effective Date:** January 2026

---

# 1. Purpose

This document establishes standards for conducting efficient, productive, and professional meetings.

Objectives:

- Improve productivity
- Reduce unnecessary meetings
- Promote collaboration
- Optimize employee time
- Improve communication

---

# 2. Scope

This policy applies to:

- Employees
- Managers
- Team leads
- Executives
- Interns
- Contractors

---

# 3. Meeting Types

The organization recognizes:

| Meeting Type | Duration |
|-------------|----------|
| Daily Standup | 15 min |
| Weekly Team Meeting | 30 min |
| Project Review | 60 min |
| Client Meeting | 60-90 min |
| Management Meeting | 90 min |
| Training Session | 60-120 min |

---

# 4. Meeting Scheduling

Before scheduling meetings:

- Verify necessity
- Check participant availability
- Define objectives
- Prepare agenda
- Set expected outcomes

Recommended notice period:

```text
Internal Meeting: 24 Hours
Client Meeting: 48 Hours
Executive Meeting: 72 Hours
```

---

# 5. Meeting Invitation Requirements

Every invitation must contain:

- Meeting title
- Objective
- Date and time
- Duration
- Agenda
- Participants
- Meeting platform

Example:

```text
Title: Sprint Planning

Objective:
Plan next sprint activities.

Agenda:
1. Review backlog
2. Estimate tasks
3. Assign responsibilities
4. Confirm deadlines
```

---

# 6. Approved Meeting Platforms

Company-approved platforms:

- Microsoft Teams
- Google Meet
- Zoom
- Slack Huddle

Personal meeting platforms are prohibited.

---

# 7. Meeting Etiquette

Participants should:

- Join on time
- Be prepared
- Mute microphones
- Use professional language
- Participate actively
- Respect others

Participants should avoid:

- Interruptions
- Multitasking
- Side conversations
- Background noise

---

# 8. Daily Standup Meetings

Daily standups should answer:

```text
1. What was completed yesterday?
2. What will be completed today?
3. What blockers exist?
```

Maximum duration:

```text
15 minutes
```

---

# 9. Project Meetings

Project meetings should cover:

- Progress updates
- Risks
- Dependencies
- Resource allocation
- Deadlines
- Escalations

Meeting minutes are mandatory.

---

# 10. Client Meetings

Employees must:

- Prepare beforehand
- Test equipment
- Join on time
- Document discussions
- Share meeting summary

Professional behavior is mandatory.

---

# 11. Meeting Recording Policy

Meetings may be recorded only when:

- Participants are informed
- Approval is obtained
- Compliance requires recording
- Training purposes exist

Unauthorized recording is prohibited.

---

# 12. Meeting Minutes (MoM)

Meeting minutes must contain:

- Date
- Time
- Participants
- Discussion summary
- Decisions
- Action items
- Owners
- Deadlines

Example:

```text
Action:
Deploy production release

Owner:
DevOps Team

Deadline:
15 January 2026
```

---

# 13. Recurring Meetings

Recurring meetings should be reviewed every quarter.

Meetings may be canceled if:

- Business value is low
- Attendance is poor
- Objectives are unclear

---

# 14. Hybrid Meetings

For hybrid meetings:

- Ensure good audio quality
- Support remote participants
- Share screens effectively
- Encourage participation

---

# 15. Meeting Room Usage

Employees must:

- Reserve rooms beforehand
- Start on time
- End on schedule
- Clean workspace
- Release unused bookings

---

# 16. Emergency Meetings

Emergency meetings may be conducted during:

- Production outages
- Security incidents
- Customer escalations
- Disaster recovery
- Business continuity events

Management approval is required.

---

# 17. Communication Channels

Approved channels:

| Channel | Purpose |
|---------|---------|
| Email | Official communication |
| Teams | Collaboration |
| Slack | Quick communication |
| Phone | Urgent communication |
| Video Call | Meetings |

---

# 18. Best Practices

Employees should:

- Keep meetings short
- Follow agenda
- Document decisions
- Assign action items
- Track progress
- Respect time limits

---

# 19. Performance Metrics

Meeting effectiveness is measured by:

- Attendance
- Completion rate
- Action item closure
- Meeting duration
- Participant feedback

Yes.
