# TechNova Solutions Pvt. Ltd.

# Employee Travel Policy

**Policy Version:** 2.1
**Department:** Operations and Human Resources
**Effective Date:** January 2026

---

# 1. Purpose

This policy defines rules and procedures governing official business travel.

Objectives:

- Ensure employee safety
- Control travel expenses
- Standardize travel approvals
- Improve operational efficiency

---

# 2. Scope

Applicable to:

- Employees
- Managers
- Executives
- Consultants
- Interns with approval

---

# 3. Travel Categories

- Domestic travel
- International travel
- Client visits
- Training travel
- Conference travel

---

# 4. Approval Process

```text
Employee
     ↓
Manager
     ↓
Department Head
     ↓
Operations Team
```

---

# 5. Flight Policy

Employees should book:

- Economy class for domestic travel
- Economy class for international travel under 6 hours
- Business class only with executive approval

---

# 6. Hotel Policy

Hotel reimbursement limits:

| Employee Level | Maximum Limit |
|---------------|---------------|
| Intern | ₹2000 |
| Engineer | ₹5000 |
| Manager | ₹8000 |
| Director | ₹15000 |

---

# 7. Transportation

Allowed transportation:

- Flights
- Train
- Taxi
- Metro
- Company vehicle

Personal vehicle usage requires approval.

---

# 8. Meal Allowance

Daily meal allowance:

| Category | Amount |
|----------|---------|
| Breakfast | ₹300 |
| Lunch | ₹500 |
| Dinner | ₹700 |

---

# 9. International Travel

Requirements:

- Passport
- Visa
- Insurance
- Approval letter

---

# 10. Expense Submission

Employees must submit:

- Boarding passes
- Hotel bills
- Receipts
- Travel report

Submission deadline:

```text
15 days after travel completion
```

---

# 11. Travel Safety

Employees should:

- Follow local regulations
- Carry emergency contacts
- Purchase travel insurance
- Inform managers of emergencies

---

# 12. Frequently Asked Questions

### Q1. Can employees upgrade flights?
No.

### Q2. Is travel insurance mandatory?
Yes.

### Q3. Is hotel reimbursement capped?
Yes.

### Q4. Can family members travel?
No.

### Q5. What is the expense submission deadline?
15 days.

---

# 13. Policy Ownership

Maintained by Operations and Human Resources Departments.
