# TechNova Solutions Pvt. Ltd.

# Procurement Process Policy

**Policy Version:** 2.0
**Department:** Operations and Finance
**Effective Date:** January 2026

---

# 1. Purpose

This policy defines the procurement process for purchasing goods, services, software, and equipment required for business operations.

Objectives:

- Ensure transparent purchasing
- Optimize procurement costs
- Maintain vendor compliance
- Standardize purchasing procedures

---

# 2. Scope

This policy applies to:

- All departments
- Permanent employees
- Procurement teams
- Finance teams
- Project managers

---

# 3. Procurement Categories

The company procures:

- Hardware equipment
- Software licenses
- Cloud services
- Office supplies
- Professional services
- Training services
- Infrastructure equipment

---

# 4. Procurement Workflow

```text
Employee Request
        ↓
Manager Approval
        ↓
Procurement Team
        ↓
Vendor Selection
        ↓
Finance Approval
        ↓
Purchase Order
        ↓
Delivery
```

---

# 5. Purchase Request

Employees must submit:

- Item description
- Quantity
- Business justification
- Budget details
- Required delivery date

---

# 6. Approval Matrix

| Purchase Amount | Approval Authority |
|----------------|-------------------|
| Below ₹10,000 | Manager |
| ₹10,000–₹50,000 | Department Head |
| ₹50,000–₹2,00,000 | Director |
| Above ₹2,00,000 | CEO |

---

# 7. Vendor Selection

Vendor selection criteria:

- Pricing
- Quality
- Delivery capability
- Compliance
- Experience
- Reputation

---

# 8. Purchase Orders

Every purchase requires:

- Purchase Order Number
- Vendor details
- Quantity
- Price
- Delivery timeline

---

# 9. Emergency Procurement

Emergency procurement may occur during:

- Production outages
- Security incidents
- Business continuity events

Director approval is required.

---

# 10. Asset Receipt

Employees must verify:

- Quantity
- Quality
- Invoice details
- Delivery documentation

---

# 11. Procurement Audits

The company may conduct:

- Internal audits
- Vendor audits
- Financial audits
- Compliance audits

---
