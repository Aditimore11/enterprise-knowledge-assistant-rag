# TechNova Solutions Pvt. Ltd.

# Payroll Policy

**Document Version:** 1.0
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026
**Department:** Human Resources & Finance
**Applicable To:** All Employees

---

# 1. Purpose

The purpose of this Payroll Policy is to establish standardized procedures for salary processing, employee compensation, payroll deductions, reimbursements, bonuses, and final settlements at TechNova Solutions Pvt. Ltd.

This policy ensures timely, accurate, and transparent payroll administration while complying with applicable labor laws and tax regulations.

---

# 2. Scope

This policy applies to:

* Full-time employees
* Permanent employees
* Probationary employees
* Contract employees (as applicable)
* Remote and hybrid employees

Interns and consultants are compensated according to their contractual agreements.

---

# 3. Payroll Cycle

The company follows a monthly payroll cycle.

| Parameter         | Details                  |
| ----------------- | ------------------------ |
| Payroll Period    | 1st to Last Day of Month |
| Salary Processing | Last Working Day         |
| Payment Method    | Bank Transfer            |
| Currency          | Indian Rupees (INR)      |

Employees must ensure their banking information remains updated.

---

# 4. Employee Compensation Structure

Employee compensation may include the following components:

## Fixed Components

* Basic Salary
* House Rent Allowance (HRA)
* Special Allowance
* Conveyance Allowance
* Medical Allowance

## Variable Components

* Performance Bonus
* Incentives
* Project Bonus
* Retention Bonus
* Sales Commission

---

# 5. Salary Credit Process

The payroll department processes salary payments through the following steps:

### Step 1

Attendance verification.

### Step 2

Leave adjustment.

### Step 3

Overtime calculation.

### Step 4

Tax deduction calculation.

### Step 5

Payroll approval.

### Step 6

Bank transfer processing.

Employees receive salary slips electronically after payroll processing.

---

# 6. Salary Deductions

Mandatory deductions may include:

* Income Tax (TDS)
* Provident Fund (PF)
* Professional Tax
* Employee State Insurance (ESI)
* Loan deductions
* Other statutory deductions

Additional deductions may apply based on employee agreements.

---

# 7. Attendance and Payroll

Payroll calculations consider:

* Employee attendance
* Approved leave
* Unpaid leave
* Overtime
* Holidays
* Absenteeism

Unauthorized absences may result in salary deductions.

---

# 8. Overtime Compensation

Overtime work must receive prior approval.

## Eligibility

Eligible employees may receive:

* Overtime payment
* Compensatory leave
* Project incentives

Overtime rates are determined according to company policy and labor regulations.

---

# 9. Performance Bonuses

Performance bonuses may be awarded based on:

* Individual performance
* Team performance
* Business performance
* Project achievements

Bonus amounts vary depending on organizational performance and management approval.

---

# 10. Annual Salary Revision

Salary reviews typically occur once every year.

Factors considered include:

* Employee performance
* Business profitability
* Market conditions
* Skills and certifications
* Internal equity

Salary increments are not guaranteed.

---

# 11. Expense Reimbursements

Employees may claim approved business expenses.

Eligible expenses include:

* Business travel
* Accommodation
* Client meetings
* Internet reimbursement
* Office supplies

Claims must include supporting documentation.

---

# 12. Leave Without Pay (LWP)

Employees who exhaust their leave balance may be placed on Leave Without Pay.

LWP deductions are calculated based on:

* Monthly salary
* Number of unpaid leave days
* Applicable benefits

---

# 13. Salary Advance

Employees may request salary advances under exceptional circumstances.

Examples include:

* Medical emergencies
* Family emergencies
* Natural disasters

Salary advances require management approval.

---

# 14. Payroll Corrections

Employees who identify payroll discrepancies should:

1. Notify Human Resources.
2. Submit supporting evidence.
3. Request payroll correction.

Corrections are processed during the current or subsequent payroll cycle.

---

# 15. Final Settlement

Final settlement includes:

* Pending salary
* Leave encashment
* Bonus eligibility
* Expense reimbursement
* Recovery of company assets

Final settlement is generally processed within 30 working days after separation.

---

# 16. Tax Compliance

The company complies with all applicable tax regulations.

Employees are responsible for:

* Providing tax declarations.
* Submitting investment proofs.
* Verifying tax deductions.
* Filing personal income tax returns.

---

# 17. Confidentiality

Payroll information is confidential.

Access is restricted to:

* Human Resources
* Finance Department
* Authorized Management Personnel

Unauthorized disclosure of payroll information is prohibited.

---

# 18. Fraud Prevention

Payroll fraud includes:

* False attendance records
* Duplicate reimbursements
* Unauthorized claims
* Misrepresentation of information

Fraudulent activity may result in disciplinary action, legal proceedings, or termination.

---

# 19. Frequently Asked Questions

### Q1. When is salary credited?

Salary is credited on the last working day of each month.

### Q2. Can employees access salary slips online?

Yes. Salary slips are available through the employee portal.

### Q3. Are bonuses guaranteed?

No. Bonuses depend on performance and business results.

### Q4. How are unpaid leaves calculated?

Salary deductions are calculated proportionally based on monthly compensation.

### Q5. Can payroll errors be corrected?

Yes. Employees should report discrepancies immediately.

---

This Payroll Policy is jointly maintained by the Human Resources and Finance Departments of TechNova Solutions Pvt. Ltd.

The company reserves the right to modify this policy to comply with business requirements and legal regulations.
