# 1. Welcome Message

Welcome to TechNova Solutions Pvt. Ltd. We are delighted to have you join our organization. This Employee Handbook serves as a guide to our company's policies, procedures, culture, and expectations. It is designed to help employees understand their responsibilities, benefits, and opportunities within the organization.

Our mission is to create innovative technology solutions while fostering a collaborative, inclusive, and growth-oriented workplace environment.

# 5. Attendance and Punctuality

Employees are expected to:

* Report to work on time.
* Record attendance using company systems.
* Inform managers of delays or absences.
* Maintain professional attendance standards.

Repeated tardiness or absenteeism may lead to disciplinary action.

---

# 6. Code of Conduct

All employees must:

* Maintain professionalism.
* Respect colleagues and clients.
* Protect company assets.
* Follow organizational policies.
* Avoid conflicts of interest.
* Maintain workplace integrity.

Employees must represent the company positively both inside and outside the workplace.

# 8. Workplace Behavior

Employees are expected to:

* Treat others respectfully.
* Maintain professionalism.
* Avoid workplace conflicts.
* Promote teamwork and collaboration.
* Report inappropriate behavior.

# 9. Confidentiality Policy

Employees may have access to confidential information including:

* Customer information
* Financial records
* Product information
* Technical documentation
* Business strategies

# 10. Information Security

Employees must:

* Protect passwords.
* Lock computer systems when unattended.
* Use approved software only.
* Report security incidents immediately.
* Follow cybersecurity guidelines.

Failure to comply may result in disciplinary action.

* Technical skills
* Communication
* Teamwork
* Leadership
* Innovation
* Business impact

# 13. Training and Development

TechNova encourages continuous learning through:

* Internal training sessions
* Online learning platforms
* Technical workshops
* Professional certifications
* Leadership development programs

Employees are encouraged to actively participate in learning initiatives.

# 14. Work From Home Policy

Employees may be eligible for hybrid or remote work arrangements subject to:

* Manager approval
* Business requirements
* Performance expectations
* Security compliance

Remote employees must remain available during official working hours.

---

# 15. Use of Company Assets

Employees are responsible for company assets including:

* Laptops
* Mobile devices
* Software licenses
* Security credentials
* Office equipment

Assets must be returned upon termination of employment.

---

# 16. Disciplinary Action

Disciplinary action may occur due to:

* Policy violations
* Misconduct
* Security breaches
* Poor performance
* Unauthorized absences

Actions may include:

* Verbal warning
* Written warning
* Suspension
* Termination

---

# 17. Employee Separation

Employees leaving the organization must:

* Provide appropriate notice.
* Complete knowledge transfer.
* Return company assets.
* Complete exit formalities.

Final settlement will be processed according to company policy.

---

# 18. Grievance Procedure

Employees experiencing workplace issues should:

1. Discuss with their manager.
2. Contact Human Resources.
3. Submit a formal grievance if necessary.

The company aims to resolve grievances fairly and confidentially.
