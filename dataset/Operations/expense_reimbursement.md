# TechNova Solutions Pvt. Ltd.

# Expense Reimbursement Policy

**Policy Version:** 2.1
**Department:** Operations and Finance
**Effective Date:** January 2026

---

# 1. Purpose

This policy establishes the procedures and guidelines for reimbursement of business-related expenses incurred by employees while performing official duties.

Objectives:

- Ensure timely reimbursements
- Maintain financial transparency
- Prevent fraudulent claims
- Standardize approval processes
- Improve operational efficiency

---

# 2. Scope

This policy applies to:

- Full-time employees
- Contract employees
- Consultants
- Interns (with approval)
- Management staff

---

# 3. Eligible Expenses

Employees may claim reimbursement for:

- Official travel expenses
- Hotel accommodation
- Client meetings
- Meals during business travel
- Office supplies
- Internet reimbursement
- Professional certifications
- Training expenses
- Local transportation
- Parking charges

---

# 4. Non-Reimbursable Expenses

The following expenses cannot be reimbursed:

- Personal shopping
- Entertainment expenses
- Alcohol
- Family travel expenses
- Traffic fines
- Personal subscriptions
- Luxury purchases
- Personal gadgets
- Vacation expenses

---

# 5. Expense Categories

| Category | Daily Limit |
|----------|------------|
| Breakfast | ₹300 |
| Lunch | ₹500 |
| Dinner | ₹700 |
| Hotel | ₹5000 |
| Transport | ₹1500 |
| Internet | ₹1000/month |

---

# 6. Travel Expense Rules

Employees should:

- Use company-approved travel vendors
- Book economy class tickets
- Select reasonable accommodation
- Retain all receipts

Allowed travel modes:

- Flight
- Train
- Taxi
- Metro
- Bus
- Company vehicle

---

# 7. Client Entertainment Expenses

Client meeting expenses are reimbursable if:

- Business purpose exists
- Manager approval is obtained
- Proper invoices are submitted

Maximum client meeting allowance:

```text
₹5000 per meeting
```

---

# 8. Training and Certification Expenses

Employees may claim:

- Course fees
- Examination fees
- Study materials
- Conference registrations

Requirements:

- Prior manager approval
- Business relevance
- Invoice submission

---

# 9. Documentation Requirements

Employees must submit:

- Original invoices
- GST bills
- Payment receipts
- Travel tickets
- Hotel invoices
- Approval emails

Claims without documentation may be rejected.

---

# 10. Submission Timeline

Employees must submit claims within:

```text
15 calendar days
```

after expense occurrence.

Late submissions require management approval.

---

# 11. Expense Claim Workflow

```text
Employee
    ↓
Reporting Manager
    ↓
Finance Team
    ↓
Operations Approval
    ↓
Payment Processing
```

---

# 12. Approval Matrix

| Amount | Approval Authority |
|--------|-------------------|
| Up to ₹5,000 | Manager |
| ₹5,001-₹25,000 | Department Head |
| ₹25,001-₹1,00,000 | Director |
| Above ₹1,00,000 | CFO |

---

# 13. Reimbursement Timeline

Approved claims are processed:

- Within 7 working days
- Through bank transfer
- During payroll processing

---

# 14. Audit and Compliance

The company reserves the right to:

- Audit claims
- Request additional proof
- Reject suspicious claims
- Conduct investigations

---

# 15. Fraud Prevention

Fraudulent activities include:

- Fake invoices
- Duplicate claims
- Inflated expenses
- False travel claims

Violations may result in:

- Claim rejection
- Salary deduction
- Disciplinary action
- Employment termination
